Daniel Carter is an associate professor of digital media innovation at Texas State University. He is the co-founder and co-director of Texas Community Health News, an organization that trains students to produce data-driven reports that have been published more than 100 times in outlets across the state.

Carter's research focuses on the social role played by data infrastructures and techniques in contexts such as journalism, government recordkeeping, marketing and humanities scholarship.

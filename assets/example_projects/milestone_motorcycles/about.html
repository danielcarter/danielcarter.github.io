Miles Stone
Owner

Miles is obsessed with everything related to motorcycles

Over 25 years ago, to the disapproval of his mother, Miles pushed home his first bike: a 1972 Honda CB125s. A friend helped him rebuild the motor, he moved out of his parent's house, and his life with motorcycles began.

Miles is also the owner of Austin Moto Academy, which offers Central Austin's only motorcycle license course approved by the Department of Public Safety and motocycle maintenance classes for different skill levels.

Dan Overly
Technician

Dan is an encyclopedia of motocycle knowledge

Dan graduated from the Motorcycle Mechanics Institute (MMI) with Top Tech honors. While at MMI, Dan received factory-approved training in BMW and Kawasaki, including motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, utility vehicles, and personal watercraft. Dan can be found traveling around Austin on either his BMW K1300s or Suzuki SV650.

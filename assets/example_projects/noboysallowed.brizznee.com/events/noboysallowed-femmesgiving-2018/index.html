FEMMESGIVING

Grab your femme + non-binary homies and get ready for #NOBOYSALLOWED’s first ever Femmesgiving celebration! Join us for a vegan potluck, music, poetry, film + art from some of our fave local femmes. Since it’s a potluck, consider bringing a dish, but trust we will have some of our fave feast foods. All donations go to Southside Community Center!

–ENTRY–
CHOOSE ONE
3 cans/ $3 / 1 dish gets you in

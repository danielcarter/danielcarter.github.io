Our Story

All American Machine is a family owned and operated machine shop that started with Ed Roberson purchasing a Clausing Engine Lathe and a Bridgeport Mill to put in his garage. Soon after, his son, Roman Roberson, began learning the machines and helped to grow the shop as a business. They have expanded the building 3 times and still plan on growing. Since then, All American Machine has worked with Weatherford, Summit, C&C Fluid Power, Excel, McKinnon Construction, and Youngs Plunger Service.

How We Are Different

We are an All American Machine Shop with All American Labor making All American Parts. The shop is founded on Roberson family values: hard work and nothing less than exceptional quality. We run all manual machines providing great quality with acute attention to detail. If you are in need of a machine shop that will give 110% into every job, All American Machine is the one to call.
